# Ficha a avaliar - versão em português

## Unidade curricular
Estratégia Empresarial

## Curso responsável
Mestrado em Engenharia de Serviços e Gestão

## Créditos ECTS
6

## Ano
2

## Semestre
1

## Objetivos
<p>This course unit aims to endow students with the basic skills to think strategically, to develop a specific business and corporate strategy, to understand the issues and hurdles of implementation, in order to play a role in a given company's competitiveness.</p>

## Resultados de aprendizagem e competências
<p>At the end of this subject, students should be able to: <br />- think strategically <br />- understand the issues and hurdles of implementing a given strategy <br />- design a specific business and corporate strategy <br />- design a strategic implementation plan <br />- define the controlling mechanisms for a given implementation plan.</p>

## Pré-requisitos e co-requisitos
<p>EIG0035 Project Appraisal EIG0036 Human Resources Management EIG0034 General Management EIG0029 Operations Management EIG0028 Total Quality Management EIG0032 Financial Management EIG0030 Logistics Management EIG0031 Marketing</p>

## Programa
<p>1. Conceitos Base</p>
<ol style="list-style-type: lower-alpha;">
<li>A empresa</li>
<li>Estratégia, Gestão estratégica, Competitividade, etc.</li>
</ol>
<p>2. Formulação Estratégica</p>
<ol style="list-style-type: lower-alpha;">
<li>As escolas de pensamento estratégico/Strategy Safari</li>
<li>Positioning model vs. resource based model</li>
</ol>
<p>3. Estratégias de Negócio</p>
<ol style="list-style-type: lower-alpha;">
<li>Revisão dos Conceitos fundamentais e das Ferramentas de análise (análise externa, interna, SWOT)</li>
<li>Revisão das estratégias tipo (custo, diferenciação, foco)</li>
<li>Cadeia de valor</li>
</ol>
<p>4. Estratégias Corporativas</p>
<ol style="list-style-type: lower-alpha;">
<li>Conceitos fundamentais</li>
<li>Diversificação/Portfolio Management</li>
<li>Integração Vertical e Outsourcing Estratégico</li>
<li>Fusões &amp; Aquisições</li>
<li>Internacionalização</li>
<li>Estratégias colaborativas</li>
</ol>
<p>5. Implementação / Execução da Estratégia</p>
<ol style="list-style-type: lower-alpha;">
<li>Conceitos fundamentais</li>
<li>Implicações para estratégia e estrutura organizacional</li>
<li>Liderança e Gestão da Mudança</li>
</ol>
6. Inovação &amp; Empreendedorismo
<ol style="list-style-type: lower-alpha;">
<li>Conceitos fundamentais</li>
<li>Gestão da Inovação</li>
</ol>

Thompson, Jr., Arthur A.; <a href="https://discovery.up.pt/permalink/351PUCS_INST/1levj0s/alma990005316600108801" title="Crafting and Executing Strategy (Abre numa nova janela)" target="_blank" >Crafting and Executing Strategy</a>. ISBN: 978-0-07-110756-3 <br>

## Métodos de ensino e atividades de aprendizagem
<p>Theoretical classes aim to endow students with the necessary conceptual, theoretical and methodological knowledge to discuss real cases in theoretical-practical classes. During the semester students have to prepare and present a case of strategic analysis based in a real company or case study. Some sessions/master classes with guest managers will take place during the semester. Attendance to the sessions/master classes is compulsory.</p>

## Tipo de avaliação
Avaliação distribuída com exame final

## Componentes de Ocupação
- Estudo autónomo: 28.0 horas
- Frequência das aulas: 56.0 horas
- Trabalho de campo: 56.0 horas
- Total:: 140.0 horas

## Fórmula de cálculo da classificação final
<p>Group Assignment (40%) + Closed book exam (60%). <br />In the Exam component, a minimum classification of 7,5 (out of 20) is required.</p>

## Obtenção de frequência
<p>Group assignment, with final presentation and final report</p>

<p>1. Basic concepts</p>
<ol style="list-style-type: lower-alpha;">
<li>The Company</li>
<li>Strategy, strategic management, competitiveness, etc</li>
</ol>
<p>2. Strategy formulation</p>
<ol style="list-style-type: lower-alpha;">
<li>Schools of strategic thinking/ strategy safari</li>
<li>Positioning model vs. resource based model based</li>
</ol>
<p>3. Business level strategy</p>
<ol style="list-style-type: lower-alpha;">

<li>Revision of strategies (cost, differentiation, focus)</li>
<li>Value Chain</li>
</ol>
<p>4. Corporate level strategy</p>
<ol style="list-style-type: lower-alpha;">
<li>Fundamental concepts</li>
<li>Diversification/ Portfolio management</li>
<li>Vertical integration and strategic outsourcing</li>
<li>Mergers &amp; Acquisitions</li>
<li>Internationalisation</li>
<li>Cooperative strategies</li>
</ol>
<p>5. Strategy implementation/execution</p>
<ol style="list-style-type: lower-alpha;">
<li>Fundamental concepts</li>
<li>Implications for the strategy and for the organizational structure</li>
<li>Leading change and change management</li>
</ol>
6. Innovation &amp; Entrepreneurship
<ol style="list-style-type: lower-alpha;">
<li>Fundamental concepts</li>
<li>Managing innovation</li>
</ol>
