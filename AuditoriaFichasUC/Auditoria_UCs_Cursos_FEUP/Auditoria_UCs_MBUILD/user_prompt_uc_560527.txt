# Ficha a avaliar - versão em português

## Unidade curricular
Gestão de Projetos de Construção (U.Porto)

## Curso responsável
Mestrado em Projeto, Construção e Gestão Sustentáveis do Ambiente Construído

## Créditos ECTS
6

## Ano
1

## Semestre
1

## Objetivos
To provide students with the knowledge and competences enabling them to understand and manage the development of a construction project, mainly in its inception and design stage.

## Resultados de aprendizagem e competências
<ul><br /><li><span style=&quot;text-align: justify;&quot;>Understand the concepts of Lean Production and Lean Construction, and apply some of its tools;</span></li><br /><li>Understand the specificities of construction industry, its life cycle, and importance of design stage;</li><br /><li>Understand that is fundamental to apply project management techniques to increase processes efficiency in construction, namely in design;</li><br /><li>Understand that cost estimation and control is one of the major problems of construction activity and it is fundamental to adopt techniques and data basis to help this estimation;</li><br /><li>Understand how digitalization is fundamental to push construction industry and what are the fundamental concerns in the design stage to achieve this objective.</li><br /></ul>

## Pré-requisitos e co-requisitos
Basic knowledge about sustainable concepts in design, construction and managements of buildings and infrastructures.

## Programa
<ol><br /><li><span style=&quot;text-align: justify;&quot;>Lean Production + Lean Construction and its approach to both design and execution activities.</span></li><br /><li style=&quot;text-align: justify;&quot;>General Construction life cycle. Understanding and relevance of design stage for the solution final performance. Development of design work. RIBA Plan of Work. Organising personal work.</li><br /><li style=&quot;text-align: justify;&quot;>Management and coordination of Teams. Project Management approaches. IPD Integrated Project Delivery. Agile Planning and SCRUM.</li><br /><li style=&quot;text-align: justify;&quot;>Deep analysis of design phase in the construction global life cycle. Requirements, design teams and deliverables. Case analysis.</li><br /><li style=&quot;text-align: justify;&quot;>Relevance of cost estimation in the design stage. Methodologies of cost estimation. Uncertainty and risks.</li><br /><li style=&quot;text-align: justify;&quot;>Construction digitalization and impacts on the design stage. Construction Information systems.</li><br /><li style=&quot;text-align: justify;&quot;>BIM Coordination. Clash detection.</li><br /><li style=&quot;text-align: justify;&quot;>Design Management and BIM.</li><br /></ol>
